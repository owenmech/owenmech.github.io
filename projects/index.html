Lyrics Visualizer

It converts any string of words into a self-similarity matrix visualization.

It will show connections from individual words when the mouse hovers over them.

It can save and download your generated images from the options menu.

Snowflakes

It produces endless procedural snowflakes, each different from the last.

It is a work in progress, but will hopefully be more interactive in the future.

It is at least fun to stare at for now.

Metro Maps

It creates a random network of transportation lines and fills them with stations .

It will generate a new map each time you click on the image.

It is also a work in progress and will be customizable down the road.

Kaleidoscope

It spawns a star every time you click on the right side of the screen.

It mirrors the bottom right section in a circle and displays the result to the left.

It will continuously bounce the objects around, creating an ever-changing kaleidoscope.

Chladni Figure

It simulates sand on a vibrating plate settling into standing wave nodes.

It generates "waves" from a 4-way symmetrical perlin noise pattern.

It will change the plate's pattern when clicked, allowing the sand to re-settle.
