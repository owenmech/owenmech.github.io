About Games Music Projects

Equinox

The soundtrack from Open Alpha's game Equinox, set in a dark and starry forest.

The track I created is "Equinox (Restored)", a remix of the title track, "Equinox".

The full album was released on all major streaming platforms in 2021, available here.

Dance Dance Retribution

The soundtrack for a thesis project by LMU animation students in 2018.

The song scores a possessed arcade game attacking an unsuspecting player.

The structure of the piece mirrors key moments in the short film.

Sinking Inn

The soundtrack behind Open Alpha's Sinking Inn, an eerie world with an imagination realm.

The tracks of mine are "Timid Tiptoe", its inversion "Safe Steps", and "Dark Waters (Reimagined)".

The latter is a remix of the title track, all of which are available to stream as of 2020 here.

Spooky Speakeasy

The soundtrack in Spooky Speakeasy from Open Alpha, a monstrous bartending game.

The songs I wrote are "Oddball", "Midnight Riff", and "Spooky Speakeasy (Reanimated)".

The sound team on this project was just two of us, and the EP is streamable here from 2020.

Check It Out!

The soundtrack to the lighthearted cashiering game Check It Out! by Open Alpha.

The track "Supermarket Bossa (Restocked)" is my jazzy remix of the title song.

The entire album can be heard since 2022 on streaming services linked here.
